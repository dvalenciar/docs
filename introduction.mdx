ReinforceUI Studio is a Python-based application that streamlines the configuration and monitoring of Reinforcement Learning (RL) training. Its intuitive GUI removes the need for extra repositories or memorizing complex command lines.
<img alt="Main Window" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/main_window.gif" />


## Why you should use ReinforceUI Studio

ReinforceUI Studio is designed to address common challenges in  (RL), making it an indispensable tool for researchers and practitioners. Here’s what sets it apart:

1. **Simplified RL Workflows**: The intuitive GUI eliminates the need for complex command-line operations
2. **Accelerated Experimentation**: Preconfigured settings, environment support, and seamless integrations reduce setup time and streamline the training process.
3. **Real-Time Feedback**: Immediate access to training metrics and performance insights helps identify trends and troubleshoot issues faster.
4. **Enhanced Collaboration**: Comprehensive logging of training data, results, and visualizations ensures reproducibility and simplifies sharing insights with your team.
5. **Flexibility for Innovation**: Whether you’re exploring standard benchmarks like MuJoCo or tuning custom hyperparameters, the platform adapts to your needs effortlessly

## Key Features of ReinforceUI Studio
- Environment Support: Seamlessly integrates with MuJoCo, [OpenAI Gymnasium](https://github.com/Farama-Foundation/Gymnasium?tab=readme-ov-file), and [DeepMind Control Suite](https://github.com/google-deepmind/dm_control).
-  Algorithm Customization: Adjust hyperparameters and algorithms or use optimized defaults for quick experiments.
- Real-Time Monitoring Dashboard: View training progress, metrics, and performance curves as they happen.
- Comprehensive Data Logging: Automatically capture training data, evaluation results, plots, models, and videos for easy post-training analysis.
- User-Friendly Training and Testing: Train, evaluate, and refine RL policies through a streamlined and intuitive workflow.
